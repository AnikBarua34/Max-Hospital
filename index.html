Our Story


Chittagong officially known as Chattogram , is a major seaport city and financial centre in southeastern Bangladesh. The city has a population of more than 25 million while the metropolitan area has a population of 4.5 million, making it the second largest city in the country . After achieving the independence in 1971 , the health sector of Bangladesh required a drastic improvement . Beside this existing Govt.Hospitals as well as private hospital can not meet the mass peoples ‘ health services with maintaining world class standard . As a result some renowned well experienced and specialist doctor’s under the leadership of Dr. Md Liakat Ali Khan was gathered to establish a new multispecialty international standard hospital in Chattogram to provide excellence health service’s for the inhabitants of greater Chattogram.
Today Max Hospital and diagnostics limited is one of the leading hospital in Bangladesh improving and providing healthcare services kind heart and the safest way possible . Located at the heart of Chattogram city, Max Hospital and Diagnostics Limited has started its journey on 1st July , 2014 with an aim to provide the best health care services . Max Hospital and Diagnostic Limited make great efforts for continuous quality improvement and comply with international standards to implement quality systems in accordance with international standards . Max Hospital and Diagnostics combines the use of the latest medical technology and highly specialized doctors in order to provide high quality treatment . We always tend to keep our patient’s health as our priority.
